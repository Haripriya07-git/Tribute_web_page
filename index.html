About Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata, an Indian industrialist and philanthropist, is the chairman emeritus of Tata Sons. He was the chairman of the Tata Group, one of India's largest conglomerates, from 1991 to 2012. Known for his visionary leadership and charitable efforts, he has left an indelible mark on India and the world.

Legacy Projects

Ratan Tata spearheaded numerous innovative projects, including the Tata Nano, the world's most affordable car, and the acquisition of iconic brands like Jaguar Land Rover. His focus on sustainability and social impact has been instrumental in shaping the future of Indian industries.
